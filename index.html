Privacy Policy — Auto Replier

Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Auto Replier ("the App") is a Facebook application that enables automatic replies to comments on Facebook Page posts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects the following data:

  • Facebook Page Access Tokens (to send replies on your behalf)
  • Facebook Page ID and Page Name
  • Comment IDs (to send private replies)

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to send automatic private replies to comments on your Facebook Page posts. We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Data is only transmitted to the Facebook Graph API on your behalf.

5. Data Storage

Page Access Tokens are stored securely in a private MongoDB database. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. User Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us at the email below. We will delete all your data within 30 days of your request.
